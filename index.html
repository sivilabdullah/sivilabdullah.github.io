Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions

What exchanges does TradeMaster AI support?

+

TradeMaster AI currently supports Binance Futures as the primary exchange. We're actively working on adding support for other major exchanges including Bybit, FTX, and Coinbase Pro in upcoming releases.

Does the bot have access to my funds?

+

No. TradeMaster AI operates using read-only API keys and executes trades through your own exchange account. Your funds always remain in your exchange account, and your API keys never leave your machine.

What crypto pairs can I trade with TradeMaster AI?

+

TradeMaster AI supports all major cryptocurrency pairs available on Binance Futures, including BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, ADA, and many others. You can easily configure which pairs you want to trade.

What is the minimum capital required to start?

+

While TradeMaster AI can work with any account size, we recommend starting with at least $1,000 to effectively use our position sizing algorithms and risk management features. The bot can also be run in paper trading mode to test strategies without risk.

How much does TradeMaster AI cost?

+

TradeMaster AI will be available in multiple pricing tiers, including a free tier with limited features. Full pricing details will be announced when we launch. Early waitlist members will receive special pricing and exclusive bonuses.
